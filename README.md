## ğŸ“Œ Project Overview
**Smart Cairo Mobility** is a data analytics project designed to monitor and visualize urban transportation trends in Cairo. The dashboard provides a 360-degree view of ride-hailing operations and metro passenger flow, helping to identify patterns in driver performance, customer behavior, and peak travel times for the years 2024 and 2025.

---

## ğŸ“Š Dashboard Features

### 1. Driver Analytics
* **KPIs:** Total drivers (100), average trips per driver, and total distance covered (~14k KM).
* **Performance:** Ranking top 5 drivers based on fares and distance.
* **Vehicle Insights:** Analysis of top car models (Hyundai, Toyota, Kia) by trip frequency.

### 2. Trip & Revenue Insights
* **Trip Trends:** Tracking 1,000 trips with a total revenue of **132,763 EGP**.
* **Route Analysis:** Identifying high-demand routes like *6th October to Zamalek*.
* **Day-Type Analysis:** Comparing fares and distances between Weekdays and Weekends.

### 3. Customer Profiling
* **Demographics:** Gender distribution (50% Male / 50% Female) and age categorization (Adults, Middle-aged, etc.).
* **Payment Preferences:** Breakdown of payments via **Wallet, Card, and Cash**.

### 4. Metro Traffic Monitor
* **Volume:** Analyzing traffic for **44.6M passengers** across 7 main stations.
* **Peak Stations:** Sadat, Ramses, and Giza recorded as the highest-traffic hubs.
